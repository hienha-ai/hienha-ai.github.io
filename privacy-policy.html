Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with our WhatsApp Business integration service. Our service is designed to automate message handling and workflow processes through n8n automation platform.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

WhatsApp Messages: Messages sent to our WhatsApp Business account, including text content, media files, and metadata (sender phone number, timestamp).

Contact Information: Phone numbers and names as provided through WhatsApp contacts.

Usage Data: Basic interaction data such as message delivery status and response times.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

• Process and respond to your messages through automated workflows

• Improve our automation services and response accuracy

• Maintain message history for context in ongoing conversations

• Ensure proper functioning of our n8n integration workflows

Data Storage and Security

Your data is processed through our secure n8n automation platform. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Message data is retained only as long as necessary for providing our services and is automatically deleted after a reasonable period unless longer retention is required by law.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Information may be shared only in the following circumstances:

• With your explicit consent

• To comply with legal obligations or valid legal requests

• To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property

Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with:

• WhatsApp Business API (Meta Platforms)

• n8n automation platform

These services have their own privacy policies which govern their use of your information.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

• Request access to your personal data

• Request correction of inaccurate data

• Request deletion of your data

• Withdraw consent for data processing

• Stop receiving messages by blocking our WhatsApp Business account
